Our Methodology

In order to build our model, we used sourced data from one of the most successful companies in the industry Lending Club.

Lending Club has on its website data extending back to 2007, which tracks up to 30 individual financial metrics. It also provides personalized interest rates charged based on those metrics

After back testing all 30 metrics we came up with a model that had the best RSQ. The factors we used were loan amount, term of the loan, annual income, FICO score, and home ownership.
